About Us

We at Lily's Cafe are committed to providing an experience rather than just a dining area. Our warm and welcoming space, which is tucked away in the centre of Nairobi, is ideal for meeting friends, savouring home-cooked treats, or unwinding with a cup of freshly brewed coffee. Our menu offers a variety of dishes prepared with love and the best ingredients, whether you're here for a quick cappuccino, a filling breakfast, or a wonderful lunch. Our welcoming staff is dedicated to making each visit memorable and making you feel completely at home. Good feelings and making memories over a hot meal and a cup of coffee are more important to us at Lily's Cafe than simply serving delicious food.
